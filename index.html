About Christina

Hi there! I'm Christina Entcheva, an NYC-based professional with experience working at tech startups, advertising agencies, and the leading sports and entertainment agency.

For years I've wanted to switch to software engineering. I've finally made the jump, quit my full-time Product Management job, and committed myself to learning to code full-time.

Before moving into Product Management, I was a Design Director, working to assure the products we put out in the world were easy to use, intuitive, and beautiful.

I'm also an artist. My animated gif artwork was recently acquired for the permanent collection at the Whitney Museum of American Art.

In my free time I'm learning to play the guitar and keyboards, playing Dungeons & Dragons with friends, and enjoy reading non-fiction, sci-fi, and classics.

I was born in Sofia, Bulgaria and currently live in Jersey City, NJ.
